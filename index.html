Who Is Indie Munich For?

Indie Munich is for musicians and performers looking for stages, collaborations, and networking opportunities. It connects venues and event hosts with talented artists for live music and open mics. Music fans can discover local gigs and support independent talent. Creatives and volunteers can gain experience in photography, videography, hosting, and sound engineering. Music students and teachers can find opportunities to learn or teach instruments like guitar, vocals, and piano.
